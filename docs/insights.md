

# Business Insights

This document summarizes the main business insights identified from the Retail Revenue Intelligence Dashboard.

## 1. Revenue and Profitability

The company generated approximately $10.38M in total revenue and $4.88M in gross profit, with a gross margin of 46.97%.

This shows that the business has a strong profitability base, but performance should still be reviewed across products, regions, and channels because high revenue does not always mean strong margin.

## 2. Regional Performance

The East region generated the highest revenue, followed by Central, South, West, and North.

This indicates that regional performance is not evenly distributed, and lower-performing regions may require further investigation around store performance, product mix, demand, or marketing effectiveness.

## 3. Product Performance

The Product Performance page shows that some products generate strong revenue, while others may require attention due to weaker margins, higher return rates, or higher discount levels.

This helps management avoid judging products by revenue alone and instead consider profitability and return behavior.

## 4. Store Performance

The Store & Region Performance page helps compare stores based on revenue, gross profit, return rate, and target achievement.

This allows operations teams to identify high-performing stores, underperforming stores, and stores that may need operational review.

## 5. Marketing ROI

Google Ads generated the highest attributed revenue, while Meta Ads delivered the strongest ROAS.

This suggests that Google Ads is strong for revenue volume, while Meta Ads is more efficient per dollar spent.

## 6. Reporting Efficiency

The project replaces manual spreadsheet-based reporting with a structured analytics pipeline and dashboard-ready data exports.

This improves consistency, reduces manual reporting effort, and gives stakeholders a clearer view of business performance.
